Backend Javascript Developer

Job brief

We are seeking a talented Backend JavaScript Developer to join our team of developers. As a Backend JavaScript Developer, you will be responsible for building and designing highly scalable services and RESTful APIs.

You will work with cutting-edge technologies in a world-class software company and collaborate with teams focusing on various exciting areas of application development, including web, mobile, and data science.

If you have a CS degree and experience in backend application development, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities

  • Build and design highly scalable services and RESTful APIs
  • Implement event-driven or message-driven architecture
  • Work on backend application development using JavaScript and related technologies
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams on various application development projects

Requirements and skills

  • CS degree or equivalent
  • Experience in building and designing highly scalable services and RESTful APIs
  • Good working knowledge of JavaScript or other object-oriented programming languages
  • Deep understanding of event-driven or message-driven architecture
  • Familiarity with some of the following frameworks: node.js, Spring Boot, Rails
  • Experience with SOA architecture and NoSQL databases is a plus

Our mission is to innovate and create technological solutions that simplify daily life while fostering deeper connections and facilitating growth in an ever-evolving digital world.
